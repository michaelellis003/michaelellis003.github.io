Sequential Interence for Hidden Markov Models

September 7, 2018

In this thesis I discuss several common sequential monte carlo methods for updating a posterior distribution as new observations are received. I used a hidden Markov model to analyze and forecast the volatility of the S & P 500. The Liu and West [2001] auxiliary particle filter is applied to sequentially update the posterior distribution of the hidden Markov model with unknown state and observation distribution parameters
