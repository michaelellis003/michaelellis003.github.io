About Me

From 2012 to 2016 I completed my undergraduate degree in applied mathematics and in 2018 I graduated with a master's degree in statistics from the University of Arkansas in Fayetteville. I took courses covering probability theory, statistical inference, time series analysis and forecasting, spatial statistics, computational statistics and machine learning algorithms. I have research experience in time series analysis. Check out my master's thesis, Sequential Interence for Hidden Markov Models!

I am interested in predictive statistical modeling and in particular I am interested in how statistical models can help us make better decisions, as well as help us better understand the world around us. I am particularly interested in time series analysis and spatial statistics. Check out some of my work on these topics and other topics in statistics and data science in my portfolio!
